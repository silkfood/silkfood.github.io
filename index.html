Menu

From hearty soups and stews to light and refreshing salads, there's something for everyone at Silk Food.

for Vegeterians

Vegetable Spring Rolls
$35
Crispy and golden spring rolls filled with a delightful mix of julienned vegetables, such as carrots, cabbage, and bean sprouts. Served with a savory dipping sauce, they are a popular and satisfying appetizer.
Vegetable Pad Thai
$30
Stir-fried rice noodles tossed with an assortment of crisp vegetables, tofu, bean sprouts, and peanuts, all coated in a tangy tamarind-based sauce. This Thai favorite offers a delightful balance of sweet, sour, and spicy tastes.
Vegetable Tempura
$30
Assorted vegetables, such as sweet potatoes, broccoli, and bell peppers, lightly battered and deep-fried to crispy perfection. Served with a dipping sauce, this Japanese dish is a delightful combination of textures and flavors.
Vegetable Biryani
$30
Fragrant basmati rice cooked with an aromatic blend of spices, mixed vegetables, and sometimes paneer (Indian cottage cheese). This flavorful Indian dish is a celebration of taste and is often served with cooling raita or chutney.

for Non Vegeterians

Chicken Biryani
$45
Fragrant Basmati rice cooked with marinated chicken, aromatic spices, saffron, and caramelized onions, creating a flavorful one-pot dish loved for its rich taste and tender meat.
Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)
$30
Tender pieces of tandoori chicken simmered in a creamy tomato-based gravy, enriched with butter, cream, and a blend of spices, resulting in a luscious and indulgent North Indian favorite.
Lamb Rogan Josh
$30
A flavorful Kashmiri curry made with succulent pieces of lamb cooked in a spicy tomato-based sauce with aromatic spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and Kashmiri red chili.
Seekh Kebabs
$30
Spiced minced meat, usually made with either lamb or chicken, molded onto skewers and grilled to smoky perfection, resulting in delicious and juicy kebabs served with chutney or naan.

Dinner

Chicken Tikka Masala
$45
Tender pieces of marinated chicken cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce with a blend of aromatic spices, creating a rich and flavorful North Indian dish often enjoyed with naan or rice.
Palak Paneer
$350
Soft paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cubes simmered in a creamy spinach gravy infused with spices, offering a delightful vegetarian option rich in flavor and nutrients.
Biryani
$30
Fragrant Basmati rice cooked with marinated chicken, lamb, or prawns, along with a variety of spices, saffron, and caramelized onions, creating a flavorful and aromatic one-pot meal.
Nihari
$30
A slow-cooked stew made with tender pieces of meat, often beef or lamb, in a spiced broth, typically served as a hearty and comforting dinner option with naan or paratha.

Coffee & Drinks

Masala Chai:
$35
A traditional Indian spiced tea made with black tea leaves, milk, aromatic spices like cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and ginger, resulting in a comforting and flavorful beverage.
Filter Coffee
$30
A popular South Indian coffee brewed with dark roasted coffee beans and served with frothy milk and sugar, offering a strong and bold flavor that's a favorite among coffee enthusiasts.
Lassi
$30
A refreshing yogurt-based drink, either sweet or salty, blended with fruits (like mango) or spices (like cumin), creating a cooling and creamy beverage perfect for warm days.
Rose Falooda
$30
A delightful and colorful drink made with rose syrup, falooda (thin vermicelli), basil seeds, and a scoop of ice cream, resulting in a sweet and fragrant concoction enjoyed as a dessert beverage.
